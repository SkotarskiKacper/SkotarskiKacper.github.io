How does one stay healthy?

Exercise

The essential part of staying healthy is regular exercise. it does not mean that you have to climb Mt.Everest every day, what matters is that you exercise frequently. Click here to learn how you can plan your exercise routine.

Here are some tips for a more effective exercise

  • Do the exercises that you are comfortble with
  • Know your limits
  • Remember about your pre and post workout meals
  • Dont forget to stretch afterwards

Healthy diet

Good eating habits are important, because a propper diet will provide your body with necessary nutrients, and allow for more efficient regeneration.

A healthy diet is a regular diet. Eating at roughly the exact same time of day will definetly make you feel better

Some things to keep in mind when following a helthy diet

  • Avoid junk food, obviously
  • Eat a full meal three times a day
  • Avoid processed foods
  • Keep in mind your calories intke
